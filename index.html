About Me

Crafting Beauty Through Art

With over 7 years of professional experience in the beauty and entertainment industry

Professional Excellence

As a dedicated makeup artist specializing in television, film, and media productions, I bring both creativity and technical precision to every project. My work spans across Sri Lanka's most prominent TV commercials, teledramas, and film productions.

I believe in enhancing natural beauty while creating authentic character transformations that serve the story. Every face tells a unique story, and my role is to help bring that narrative to life on screen.
